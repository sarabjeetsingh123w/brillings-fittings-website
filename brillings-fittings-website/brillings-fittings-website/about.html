About Us

Our Company

"Quality is our mandate."

Brillings Fittings Inc. is Ontario's leading designer, developer, and manufacturer of Process Equipment Fittings, Oil and Gas Equipment (Surface and Down-Hole), Industrial and Medical Fittings.

Founded in 1991 by Jack and Tracy Brillings, Brillings Fittings started out in a 4,000 square foot facility. With over 200 employees and a brand new 20,000 square foot facility, Brillings Fittings Inc. is poised to continue its rapid growth while maintaining its unblemished reputation for quality.

Brillings Fittings Inc. achieved ISO 9001 certification in 1996. We guarantee the quality of our products in writing!
